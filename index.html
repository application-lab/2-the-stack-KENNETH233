Negative Impact of A Digital Life: Users Addiction

Users layer: For users, opening social application out of a habit is gradually formed through the constant process of using Apps for leisures. We spend too much time doing nonsense on social media application.

Interface layer: Interface design is also one of the key elements that cause the unconsciousness of using Apps of users. Easy to use, just scroll down, random click, etc. Those simple design require no intellectual operation, which means that people can use it without using their brain. Using Apps gradually become repeated, same-operation, non-intellectual work and users will fall into addiction.

Address layer: Communication is important in our life. Address layer may makes people pay attention to their phone: whose message I just get? Did I successfully send the message out or not? Those worried and concern can deeply affect our life.

City layer: Mobile services company like China mobile and China Telecome will provide users stable, high-speed Internet services, which is essential for mobile social media applicaiton. Because of the high-speed and stable services, people are glad to use their mobile Apps. Imaging this: If you can only see the loading icon of Instagram all the time, you will not be addicted to it.

Cloud layer: Cloud storage may record users behavior and personal information. Based on these data, the Apps can provide more content fit for every individual so as to build user stickiness.
